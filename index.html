About Us

Socioturtle is a community-driven platform that sparks curiosity and meaningful conversations. It brings people together to learn, share knowledge, and grow lifelong through thoughtful discussions.

Our Story

SocioTurtle was founded by Poonam Pal, a technology professional with nearly two decades of experience in the IT industry. Through her journey across sectors, she realized that while digital platforms connect people, they rarely encourage meaningful conversations and deeper learning.

Built on the belief that curiosity and shared wisdom strengthen communities, SocialShop creates a space where people can ask questions, exchange ideas, and learn from diverse perspectives.
